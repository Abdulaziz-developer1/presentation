Welcome to our Presentation "Crime and punishment"

Crime & Punishment explores the complex struggle between guilt, redemption, and the law. It takes you deep into the minds of those who commit crimes and the systems that punish them. Through this story, we examine what drives people to make difficult choices and the consequences that follow.

Tenses

Present
